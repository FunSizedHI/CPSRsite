About

The Community for Peace and Spiritual Renewal (CPSR) at Spalding University

CPSR facilitates individual and collective journeys towards spiritual renewal, inward liberation, and social transformation. We seek to do this by drawing from the timeless wisdom of Howard Thurman.


CPSR Vision

All Spalding University faculty, staff, and students are grounded in spiritual values which anchor and consistently support us in boldly and compassionately living out our Spalding University mission through the promotion of peace and justice within and beyond our community.


CPSR Mission

CPSR is dedicated to facilitating the exploration and cultivation of spiritual values and disciplines which anchor and liberate us to boldly and compassionately live out our mission through the promotion of peace and justice within and beyond our Spalding community.

Who Are We?

Located at Spalding University (SU), CPSR is a community of folks (currently made up of SU students, faculty, and staff) committed to providing ongoing, meaningful, creative shared experiences that ground our diverse community of learners in spiritual values and restorative practices which promote peace and justice.

We seek to accomplish this primarily on SU's campus before shifting our focus outward to the rest of Louisville. Furthermore, we believe that positive change is brought about through the ongoing processes of Personal Spiritual Renewal, Inward Liberation, and Social Transformation.


Inward Journey

Transcendent journeys of personal spiritual renewal increase our understanding of and ability to manage: personal commitments, spiritual values, purpose, and strengths. These allow us to experience greater peace within self and ultimately be freed to promote peace and justice with others and the broader community.


Outward Journey

Transactional journeys of social transformation help us deepen our understanding of the "needs of the times," appreciate and leverage differences, build on commonalities, and exercise spiritual disciplines and commitments to promote peace and justice in the broader community.

Spalding University Mission

We are a diverse community of learners dedicated to meeting the needs of the times in the tradition of the Sisters of Charity of Nazareth through quality undergraduate and graduate liberal and professional studies, grounded in spiritual values, with emphasis on service and the promotion of peace and justice.
