Who Are We?

Situated at Spalding University (SU), CPSR is a community of folks (currently made up of SU students, faculty, and staff) committed to providing ongoing, meaningful, creative shared experiences that ground our diverse community of learners in spiritual values and restorative practices which promote peace and justice.

We seek to accomplish this primarily on SU's campus before shifting our focus outward to the rest of Louisville. Furthermore, we believe that positive change is brought about through the ongoing processes of Personal Spiritual Renewal, Inward Liberation, and Social Transformation.
